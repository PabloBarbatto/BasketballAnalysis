Machine Learning and NBA Player Performance

NBA Shot Success Prediction

This project is dedicated to exploring machine learning using Keras and a Neural Network to predict shot accuracy of individual NBA players during the 2017-2018 season. Simply pick a player and plot their shot origin position to explore their odds of success. Go head to head with players of your choice in displays that compare performance.

Take Your Shot

It's the bottom of the fourth period, the seconds are ticking down. The home team crown is on the edge of their seats; the game is tied. There's enough time left for one last play...

Pick your player, the spot they will shoot from, and cross your fingers. The shot predictor will do the rest.

Explore the Data

In the course of the machine learning exercise there was a significant amount of data that could be explored in various ways. Using tableau we can offer more insights into trends and patterns into player performance.

Explore the dashboards by clicking tabs and manipulating the player filters.
